About
Analytics That Actually Drive Action

I'm a Data Analyst based in Fort Worth, Texas, with 8+ years of experience in business intelligence, reporting, and dashboard development. For the last 5 years, I've been deep in the retail e-commerce and beverage alcohol industry through Bottlecapps, where I work with large-scale transactional datasets containing tens of millions of records.

My strength isn't just building dashboards — it's understanding the business question behind the data and designing solutions that teams actually use to make decisions. From campaign attribution to store lifecycle tracking, I focus on turning complex datasets into clear, story-driven insights.

I collaborate with cross-functional teams — marketing, operations, sales, and leadership — to deliver analytics that shape strategy, optimize operations, and uncover growth opportunities.
